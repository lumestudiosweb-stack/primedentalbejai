OUR CARE SERVICES

New Standards in Aesthetic Care.

Digital Outlook

3D visualisation of your teeth using state-of-the-art intraoral scanners and advanced radio imaging.

Hollywood Smile

Personalized cosmetic smile designing and makeovers, using premium ceramics and composite veneers harmonizing your natural aesthetics.

Microscopic Endodontics

In-house single visit root canal specialists using microscopic Loupes for precise and hassle-free accurate treatment.

Digital Implants

Restoring missing teeth with digitally guided single and full mouth, dental implant placements with the help of CBCT as well as intraoral scans. Restoring functionally balanced Occlusions.

Orthodontics

Alignment of crooked and malaligned teeth with Invisalign and invisible braces as well as conventional orthodontics.

Expert Surgical Care

Procedures which include impacted 3rd molar extractions, cyst enucleations apicectomies, flap surgery, receding gum line corrections using laser.

Single Day Ceramics

Immediate results with single day dental ceramics, veneers and CAD milled zirconia crowns, which are delivered to you within 24 hours to respect your busy schedule and insured zirconia crown deliveries with warranties.

For Pediatric and Restorative Dentistry

Comprehensive pediatric, preventive, and restorative care focused on comfort and early prevention. We use minimally invasive techniques, including rubber dam isolation, to deliver safe, precise, and natural-looking restorations for all ages.

New Standards In Aesthetic Care

Because your smile should never be an insecurity, we combine dental aesthetic expertise with a comprehensive approach with advanced smile designing softwares for veneers, laminates and full mouth rehabilitation.
